Installation

Fire doors are a very important safety feature of any building, fire doors prime purpose is to restrict the spread of fire and smoke, and to save lives. It is legally required under the regulatory Reform (Fire safety) order that all fire doors are kept efficiently and can be easily repaired.

It is imperative that fire doors are routinely inspected and maintained to guarantee their efficiency should a fire happen. An efficient fire door can save lives; therefore if regulations are not followed through correctly, it can result in prosecution and large fines for the building managers and landlords.
