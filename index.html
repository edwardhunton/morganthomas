About Us

Fire Door London is a part of the Morgan Thomas Carpentry and Joinery Group. Fire Door London specialises in the supply, installation and upgrade of fire rated doors to meet current fire regulations within all types of properties including industrial, commercial, educational and residential.

We offer a free no obligation quote and options of standard sizes or made to measure and we pride ourselves on recommendations from clients and our strong on-going reputation of high standards and quality of work.

The importance of our fire doors and their installation need to be recognised as very important, therefore Fire Door London make sure they are correctly installed by trained specialists to the highest standard.
