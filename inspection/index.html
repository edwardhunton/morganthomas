Inspection

For guaranteed efficiency, fire doors need to be subject to regular maintenance inspections. Fire Door London provides a service of on-going regulation compliance.

Since it became a legal requirement in 2005 under the Regulatory Reform (Fire safety) Order, fire doors are subject to a suitable system of maintenance. Fire Door London will supply a detailed report of each door within the premises where necessary, with a full and detailed breakdown of what works is required to ensure you are fully complying with the regulations.
